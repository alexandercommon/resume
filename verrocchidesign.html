Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for a renovation project?

Most projects take between 8 and 16 weeks from initial consultation through final walkthrough, depending on project scope.

Do you provide design services before construction?

Yes. Our design team delivers detailed plans, 3D renderings, and material selections for client approval prior to any construction work.

How do you handle budgeting and cost control?

We provide transparent, line‑item estimates and log any change orders for client approval before proceeding.

Are permits and inspections included?

Yes. We obtain all required permits and coordinate inspections to ensure compliance with local building codes.

What warranties do you offer on completed work?

We provide a one‑year workmanship warranty on all labor and coordinate manufacturer warranties on materials and fixtures.

Can I stay in my home during the renovation?

In most cases you can remain on site; we will establish safety zones and a temporary path to minimize disruption.

What is the payment schedule and terms?

We require a 20% deposit at contract signing, progress draws at key milestones, and the balance upon final walkthrough.
